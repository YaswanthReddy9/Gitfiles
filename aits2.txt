Annamacharya Institute of Technology & Sciences, Rajampet Overview
Annamacharya Institute of Technology & Sciences is situated in Rajampet in Andhra Pradesh state of India. Established in 1998, it is accredited from NBA, NAAC, AICTE and it is affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University. AITS RAJAMPET, Rajampet offers 18 courses across 3 streams namely Engineering, IT, Management and across 4 degrees like B.Tech, M.Tech, MBA, MCA. AITS RAJAMPET campus is spread over 30 Acres.
